About us

We named our company after one of the highest mountains in the world, Carstensz, a.k.a Puncak Jaya Wijaya, located in Papua, Indonesia. It is one of the toughest peak to climb and, therefore, one of the most prestigious peak to conquer.

A mountain is the symbol of achievement, strength, stability, pride, boldness, courage and fortress, which are the spirits we want to transform into our company’s work cultures and commitment. A spirit to always strive to achieve the highest possible goal with courage to do differently – yet cautious, commitment to complete every engagement, with the highest standard of integrity and professionalism. A mental stage of which our employees applied on every tasks and assignments, internally or externally, without fail.

A strong client focus, an emphasis on teamwork, unwavering integrity, mutual respect and a constant striving for excellence are at the core of Carstensz Sinergi Indonesia. We are driven to help our clients succeed. In every interaction and with every solution, we act in our clients’ best interests striving to understand their needs, respecting their perspectives and exceeding their expectations.

We bring innovative solutions and world class advice to our clients by working across boundaries of business, geography and function. We help each other succeed and create more value by working together.

We realize our clients invest more than their time and money with us; they also invest their trust. We seek to earn that trust every day through professionalism, doing what is right and telling the truth. We are accountable to the organizations and people with which we interact – including clients, shareholders, regulators and each other for our actions and results.

Vision and Mission

To be a prominent, trusted and recognized professional business consultant in Indonesia and in Southeast Asia, by providing an unmatched business solutions and approach, for both government and private business sector.

Company Structure

Engaged in high profile business consulting and working with government to provide community business development consultation, and by focusing on specific areas of our expertise; Health & Safety Environment, Business Development, University Accreditation, and HR Restructuring & Development.
