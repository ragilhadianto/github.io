Financial Restructuring

The ultimate goal of a business is to achieve a certain financial level. However, this ultimate goal is often treated with the least attention in a company. They are busy on many operational aspects until they realize they don’t have a good financial structure to finance their operations.

FINANCIAL REPORTING

All business achievements will, at the end, be measured by money, which will be reflected on financial reports. Investors will evaluate all businesses from its financial reports. We provide a service to prepare and develop your business’ auditable financial reports according to International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS).

CASH FLOW MANAGEMENT

Not every CEO or business owner has finance education background. While it is very crucial to for a company, no matter the size of your business, to have a good cash flow management, to ensure that your day to day operations, your short term to long term business plan, will be sufficiently funded on due course.

FINANCIAL MODELLING

A good cash flow management must be supported by a good financial modeling. We can provide you with latest financial modeling that is practical, free of useless financial jargons.
