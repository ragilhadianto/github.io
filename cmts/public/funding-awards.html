Funds

Sort :

To date, CM has as PI won 12 research grants from O Fundo para o Desenvolvimento das Ciências e da Tecnologia de Macau (FDCT), National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), University of Macau Research Committee as well as several Mainland universities.
One notable example is an FDCT-NSFC joint sponsorship in 2019. These generous supports have enabled the thriving of ‘Chun Ming Tong Sui’, a young and vibrant team devoted to weaving sweet polymer chains into new medicines.
