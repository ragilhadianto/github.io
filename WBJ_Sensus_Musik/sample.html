About

Joining forces with IDGAF as one of the biggest multi platform streaming festivals in the country, Whiteboard Journal presents an online festival that focuses on showcasing music and discussions that will lead you to a fresh start into the future.

The festival will build around two of Whiteboard Journal’s signature programs: Loka Suara Live, a live music performance with special acts, and Screen Time, online chit-chat with a variety of guests.

From Jirapah to Isyana Sarasvati, Abenk Alter to Sarah Deshita, we will close this year with one of a kind music performances and ideas that will help us start the year 2021 with a fresh perspective.

Loka Suara Live

Live music performances with special acts from Jirapah, Efek Rumah Kaca, Isyana Sarasvati and Diskoria. Each performing a unique set featuring the likes of Rara Sekar to M.M.S. (Mela, Mar, Sari), with visuals from VNGNC, only in IDGAF by Whiteboardjournal.com.

Screen Time

Online chit-chat with a variety of guests to summarize what we have learned from 2020, and how can we better prepare for 2021. Inviting credible and relevant figures such as Abenk Alter, Saleh Husein, Sammy Bramantyo, Sarah Deshita, to Muklay, these sessions will be insightful yet entertaining for all.
