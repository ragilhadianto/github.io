Privacy Policy

Davis Materialworks takes your privacy very seriously. We ask that you read this privacy policy (the “Policy”) carefully as it contains important information about what to expect when our website collects personal information about you, and we will use your personal data.

This Policy applies to information we collect on davismw.com; dmw.sg; supercharge.business and its sub-domains about:

  1. Visitors to our websites (the “Site”) or sub-domains.
  2. Visitors who use our website services.
  3. People who register their emails with us or contact us with their contact information via our websites.
  4. People who make payments to us using our websites/solutions.

 

1. INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM ALL VISITORS TO OUR WEBSITE

We will obtain personal data about you when you visit us. When you visit us, we may monitor the use of this Site through cookies and similar tracking devices. For example, we may monitor the number of times you visit our Site or which pages you go to. This information helps us to build a profile of our users. Some of this data will be aggregated or statistical, which means that we may not be able to identify you individually. Please see further the section on “Our Use of Cookies” below.


2. INFORMATION COLLECTED FROM USERS WHO REGISTER THEIR CONTACT INFORMATION WITH US

2-1. When you register your contact information with us or provide your contact information to us, we may use them for the following:

  • Send you marketing materials or contact you about products and services we think you may be interested in.
  • Send you marketing or contact you about our partners' product or services we think you may be interested in.


You agreed with these by using our website services and voluntarily submitting your personal information to us. By visiting our sites or using our website services, you have given your consent that Davis Materialworks can use the information given as set out in this policy. You can always opt-out at a later date if you would rather not receive this information in the following ways;

  • By Email / Sms: You can opt out using the unsubscribe option. We will always have to unsubscribe options in most of our marketing emails or SMSes.
  • By Writing: You can write to us to unsubscribe. Please provide clear information about yourself and contact you wish to opt-out, then mail to 39 Ubi Road 1 #02-04, Singapore 408695.


3. OUR USE OF COOKIES

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behaviour information. The information is used to track visitor use and compile statistical reports on Site activity. For further information about cookies, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org. You can set your browser not to accept cookies and the above websites tell you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, some of our Site features may not function in a few cases if you remove cookies from your browser.


4. HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

We are committed to maintaining the security of personal information. We have put appropriate security procedures and technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information, such as saving guarding with sophisticated IT equipment and data management.


5. ACCESS TO YOUR INFORMATION AND UPDATING AND CORRECTING YOUR INFORMATION

Subject to the exceptions referred to in section 21(2) of the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) of Singapore (“PDPA”), you have the right to request a copy of the information that we hold about you. If you would like a copy of some or all of your personal information, please send an e-mail with an e-mail address to return to or send a written letter with your name and address to return to. There will be a small administration charge for providing you with this service.

We want to ensure that your personal information is accurate and up to date. If any of the information that you have provided to us changes, for example, if you change your email address, name, or payment details, or if you wish to cancel your registration, please let us know the correct details by sending an email by using our website contact form www.davismw.com/contact-us (choose General Enquiry / Enquiry) or by sending a letter to :


Data Administrator of Davis Materialworks
Address: 39 Ubi Road 1 #02-04, Singapore 408695.

Note: You may ask us, or we may ask you, to correct information you or we think is inaccurate, and you may also ask us to remove inaccurate information.


6. NON-DISCLOSURE

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to third parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you on behalf of Davis Materialworks, as long as these parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our and others’ rights, property, or safety. However, we may provide non-personally identifiable visitor information to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.


7. LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES

Our Site may contain links to other websites. This privacy policy only applies to websites managed by us. We recommend that you read the website's privacy policies when you visit as they are not managed by us and may have a different set of policies from ours.


8. SALE OF BUSINESS

Suppose our business is sold or integrated with another company. In that case, your details may be disclosed to our advisers and any prospective purchasers and their advisers and will be passed on to the business's new owners.


9. CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY

We keep our privacy policy under regular review. If we change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page and place notices on other pages of the Site so that you may be aware of the information we collect and how we use it at all times. This privacy policy was last updated on 29 Nov 2021.


10. HOW TO CONTACT US

We welcome your views about our website and our privacy policy. If you would like to contact us with any queries or comments, please use our website contact form www.davismw.com/contact-us (choose General Enquiry / Enquiry).
