ABOUT US

Based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, we are a member of Le & Tran, premier boutique litigation firm, but focus only on the practice of family law. We pride ourselves to be a sole law firm in Vietnam that are exclusively equipped with specialized knowledge & experience in family law to bring a unique effective solution to clients.

Like no others, our experienced attorneys are accompanied with Senior litigators who bring in practical and effective solution to avoid any potential litigation dispute where clients do not wish to escalate. Our clients benefit from our diverse team of dedicated legal professionals, working together to achieve successful results while always focusing on our clients’ well-being.


We take the time to understand the circumstances of clients’ case and work diligently toward their desired outcomes. We pride ourselves on providing honest advice and timely communication. You can count on our attorneys and client support team to exceed your expectations.


Our family law lawyers are highly respected for their mastery of the field of family law and deep understanding of the legal nuances that sometimes can make or break a complicated case. Our lawyers are known not only for their abilities to skillfully litigate contested matters, but also for the most effective use of their experience, knowledge and sound judgment to achieve negotiated settlements.
