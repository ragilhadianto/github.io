Overview Stephen is the senior trial lawyer and founder of Le & Tran and its “Family Lawyers” branch. With years of extensive experience, Stephen has impressive litigation, arbitration, and criminal defense background and a wealth of knowledge and a deep understanding of how Vietnam’s legal system, courts, and authorities operate. He has pursued the philosophy of “Fighting for Justice” for his entire illustrious career.

In 2017, Stephen was the Winner of the prestigious Disputes Star of the Year awarded by AsiaLaw Profiles in Hong Kong. He has followed up this achievement by receiving numerous additional accolades, including being endorsed as a preeminent trial lawyer of Vietnam by over 50 global corporations. He has been highly ranked in Chambers Asia Pacific, Legal500, Benchmark Litigation, and AsiaLaw Profiles for consecutive years. Large multinational enterprises such as Petronas (Malaysia) endorsed Stephen as a top-ranked lawyer in Vietnam.
