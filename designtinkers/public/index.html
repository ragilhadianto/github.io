Introduction

DesignTinkers recognise the child’s unique ways of seeing the world. We want them to remain inquisitive of what works and what does not in their environment and to instil critical thinking at an early age. By exposing children to design concepts, principles and aesthetics, we introduce design as a process to problem solve and be creative in finding solutions.

Holiday Programmes

We at DesignTinkers provide fun & nurturing camps and workshops crossing between Art, Design & Architecture. Our programmes are designed to pique curiosity, prompt inquiries, challenge norms, test ideas to get to unique outcomes through a design process.

Who We Are

An educator, an architect, a data analyst, experimental parents with varied interests in art, culture, language and design. We value diversity, innovation, preservation and sustainability.

Tessa Ng

Tessa received her BA in Architecture degree from RMIT University and is a registered Architect (ARBV) with professional practice experience across Australia and Singapore. In 2010, Tessa embarked on her Architectural teaching journey and have since developed a passion and keen interest to work with teens and children.

Extending that knowledge and experience, Tessa co-founded DesignTinkers to provide a fun and nurturing space for the young; to promote creativity and critical thinking through exploring design ideas and solutions via hands-on making and creating.

Esther Wang

Esther started out in the corporate world as a market and consumer insight analyst for most part of her career and she had a short stint with the National Arts Council. Esther started a family and has a young child in elementary school, and she has since been actively engaged in activities that involve children’s development. In her free time, she picked up a foreign language and speaks it fluently.

​​Esther values very much the benefits of art education for children and aspires to provide an avenue for children to express their creativity in thinking, imagining and exploring. She believes that a child’s eagerness to design, make art and showcase their creations reinforces a child’s sense of achievement at an early age which builds great self-esteem in kids to take pride in their work.

Esther constantly scours for fun programs to help grow a child’s artistic confidence and this steadfastly led to co-founding DesignTinkers; to expand the vision of unlocking each child’s innate creativity through exploratory play.

Partnerships + Collaborations

Schools Partnerships

We can curate co-curricular programmes, enrichment courses and holiday programmes for your students that would also suit your needs. Feel free to contact us and we look forward to developing a program together with you.

Workshop Collaborations

DesignTinkers collaborated with Creative Learning Experience, London, for a 4-day online Holiday Workshop to Re-imagine Our Home Objects. During the sessions, children were encouraged to observe and be sensitive to the design of objects and spaces around them. They were to critique what could work better in their environment and to re-invent, re-design their chosen objects before producing rapid prototypes to test their ideas.

DesignTinkers are always open to collaborations to test ideas and develop solutions with children in a workshop. Feel free to contact us to discuss the possibilities of working together.
