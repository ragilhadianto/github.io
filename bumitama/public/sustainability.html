Sustainability

Environtment

We are committed to conserving natural resources and protecting

Community

Integrating our operations through partnerships with local communities is crucial to the way we operate. With 112 villages surrounding our operations, we build on the principles of inclusion and adopt a participatory approach in managing land and natural resources.

Workplace

We employ over 31,000 people across our operations and their safety, job satisfaction, and productivity is our utmost priority.

Responsible Sourcing

We demand traceability through all our operations and although this poses unique and difficult challenges, we have achieved 98.8% traceability to plantation for our FFB.

Certification

Although, we strive above the requirements from certification schemes we work with a number of different groups, such as the RSPO, ISPO, and ISO to ensure our operations are sustainable for the planet, and for our communities for many generations to come.

Policies and Reports

As a responsible grower in Indonesia, we Bumitama recognises that the palm oil industry must address concerns on deforestation, climate change and social injustice.
