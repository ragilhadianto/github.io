Bumitama Agri acquires 3,642 Ha of land in Lower Perak

Shaping a secure and sustainable energy future for all

Bumitama Agri Ltd. is one of the leading producers of palm oil and palm kernel in Indonesia. We have been listed on the Singapore Stock Exchange since 2012 and have operations in Riau, and Central and West Kalimantan. We strive to achieve excellence through being disciplined and endeavour to lead the industry through continuous improvement.
